About Caffeine Decay Simulator

The STIMCURVE intelligence engine simulates your body's systemic metabolic decay of caffeine. It calculates exactly how long a stimulant will persist in your neural pathways, helping you time your intake to perfectly protect your natural sleep architecture.

Key Features

  • Precision Decay Math: Uses 5-hour half-life algorithms.
  • Visual Projection: Generates a full 24-hour decay curve chart.
  • Sleep Risk Indicator: Calculates active milligrams at target bedtime.
  • Local Processing: 100% private, executed securely in your browser.
  • Export Capability: Download your kinetic data to CSV instantly.

How to Use

  1. Enter your planned Intake Time.
  2. Input the Caffeine Dose in milligrams (e.g., Coffee = 95mg, Energy Drink = 150mg).
  3. Set your ideal Target Bedtime.
  4. Click "Initiate Stimulation Analysis" to map the curve.

Who It's For

Biohackers, students, shift workers, and professionals looking to optimize energy without compromising deep sleep phases.
