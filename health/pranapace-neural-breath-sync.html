About This Tool

The Guided Breathing Timer is an advanced, privacy-first wellness application designed to rapidly regulate your nervous system. By utilizing scientifically proven breathing patterns like the Box Protocol (4-4-4-4) and Calm Protocol (4-7-8), it helps lower cortisol levels, improve cognitive focus, and induce a state of deep relaxation entirely within your browser.

Key Features

  • Multiple Protocols: Pre-loaded with Box, Calm, and Awake breathing patterns.
  • Visual Biofeedback: Morphing geometry seamlessly guides your inhale, hold, and exhale phases.
  • 100% Free & Private: No signup required, and all processing happens locally on your device.
  • Session Tracking: Built-in forensic metrics to track your completed cycles and session uptime.
  • Zero Distractions: Minimalist interface optimized for pure focus and meditation.

How to Use

  1. Click the Initiate Breath Protocol button to start the engine.
  2. Select your desired breathing technique from the settings icon (bottom right).
  3. Sync your breathing with the central orb: inhale as it expands, exhale as it shrinks.
  4. Follow the textual visual cues (Inhale, Hold, Exhale) displayed at the top.
  5. Use the play/pause button to pause the session at any time.

Who It's For

Ideal for students needing study focus, professionals dealing with high-stress environments, athletes for recovery, or anyone seeking a quick, accessible mindfulness tool to combat daily anxiety.
