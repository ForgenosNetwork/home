What is NEUROFLEX?

NEUROFLEX is an advanced cognitive conditioning engine based on the psychological Stroop effect. It is designed to train mental agility, improve response inhibition, and accelerate executive function by forcing your brain to resolve conflicting data streams in real-time.

Key Features

  • Instant browser-based processing
  • Real-time cognitive metric tracking
  • Adaptive momentum pacing mechanism
  • 100% private and secure (no data stored)
  • Cross-platform mobile and desktop support
