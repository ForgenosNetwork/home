About the Idea Clustering Engine

This tool automatically analyzes your raw thoughts and brainstorms, using semantic similarity to group them into an interactive visual mind map. Find hidden patterns and connections in your data instantly without creating an account.

Key Features

  • Instant semantic clustering of text
  • Force-directed visual mind mapping
  • 100% private in-browser processing
  • No signup, login, or watermarks
  • Export clusters easily to text files

How to Use

  1. 1 Dump a raw thought or idea into the input field.
  2. 2 Click Store Idea to add it to your local network.
  3. 3 Repeat this process for all your scattered brainstorms.
  4. 4 Click Initiate Cultivation to analyze and group them.
  5. 5 Explore the visual map and export your organized clusters.
