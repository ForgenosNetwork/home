What this tool does

The Advanced Image Blur tool allows you to securely redact, pixelate, or obscure sensitive information in any image. Processing happens entirely in your local browser, guaranteeing absolute privacy as your files never leave your device.

Key Features

  • Custom brush and shape selection tools
  • Multiple effects: Gaussian, Pixelate, Frosted, Motion
  • 100% Client-side processing (Zero server uploads)
  • Real-time preview with Before/After slider
  • High-resolution lossless PNG export

How to Use

  1. Upload your image: Drag and drop any JPG, PNG, or WEBP file into the upload zone above.
  2. Select a tool: Choose a freehand brush or a shape selection tool from the top toolbar.
  3. Adjust settings: Tweak blur intensity, brush size, and edge softness in the right panel.
  4. Paint to obscure: Click and drag over the areas you wish to hide.
  5. Export: Click Download to save the perfectly censored image to your device.
