    CHRONOBLOCK

    Tactile Time Allocation Matrix — 24 hours, physical blocks, zero compromise.

    How to Use the Time Blocker

    This visual schedule planner forces realistic daily planning by applying physical block constraints. You only have 24 hours in a day—allocate them wisely to maintain balance and avoid cognitive overload.

    Key Features

    • Strict 24-hour limit prevents overbooking
    • Drag-and-drop tactile block interface
    • Automatic cognitive load calculation
    • Fatigue and burnout risk warnings
    • 100% private, browser-based storage

    How to Use

    1. 1. Drag a 1-HR block from the dock to a time slot.
    2. 2. Type a label for the block (e.g., "Deep Work", "Break").
    3. 3. Monitor Focus vs. Break hours in the insight panel.
    4. 4. Click 'Lock Day Structure' to finalize your matrix.

    Who It's For

    Perfect for students planning rigorous study blocks, freelancers tracking client work sessions, developers managing sprint tasks, and anyone looking to optimize their daily routine efficiently without the clutter of traditional calendars.
