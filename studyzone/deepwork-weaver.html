What is Deepwork Weaver?

Deepwork Weaver is an advanced cognitive focus tracking tool designed to gamify and protect your productivity. By creating a visual "fabric" of your work session, it incentivizes distraction-free work blocks and logs interruptions to help you build unbreakable focus habits.

Key Features

  • Visual Focus Weaving: Watch your focus build a digital tapestry in real-time.
  • Distraction Logging: Instantly log interruptions and see their impact on your focus integrity.
  • Streak Engine: Maintain consecutive perfect days to unlock 'Masterpiece Mode'.
  • 100% Local & Private: All data processing happens securely within your browser.

How to Use

  • 1. Set your desired Deep Work block duration (5 - 240 mins).
  • 2. Click "Initiate Deep Work Sequence" to begin the session.
  • 3. Work without breaking focus. If distracted, click "Log Interruption".
  • 4. Complete the timer to save your weave and build your focus streak.

Who It's For

Perfect for developers, students, writers, and professionals who struggle with context-switching, digital distractions, or maintaining long periods of high-cognitive output.
