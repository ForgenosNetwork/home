VELOCITYREAD

Neural RSVP Cognition Engine. Accelerate reading speed and eliminate eye movement inefficiencies.

300

Accelerate Your Cognition

VELOCITYREAD is an advanced Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP) engine. It eliminates eye saccades (movement) by presenting words sequentially at their Optimal Recognition Point (ORP), allowing you to read up to 3x faster with maximum comprehension.

Core Features

  • Zero Eye Fatigue: Words center on the optimal focal pivot point.
  • Dynamic Pacing: Automatically pauses on long words & punctuation.
  • 100% Private: Engine runs entirely within your local browser.
  • Custom WPM: Modulate processing speeds from 100 to 1000 WPM.

How to Use

  1. Paste Text: Insert your article or document into the input field above.
  2. Set Speed: Adjust the WPM (Words Per Minute) cognitive slider.
  3. Initiate: Click the large action button to begin processing data.
  4. Focus: Stare at the pulsing red letter in the center of the screen.
