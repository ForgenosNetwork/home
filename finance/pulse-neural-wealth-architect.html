What is PULSE Architect?

PULSE Neural Architect is an advanced probability engine designed to visualize the long-term, compounding impact of daily micro-spending habits. By utilizing Monte Carlo simulations, it projects potential financial outcomes, revealing the true "Regret Delta" of small daily expenses over a decade.

Key Features

  • Forensic financial growth mapping over 10 years
  • 500+ Iteration Monte Carlo Cloud Simulation
  • Dynamic probability and risk-tolerance analysis
  • 100% private, browser-based offline processing

How to Use

1

Set your baseline: Adjust your current age, available capital, and personal risk index in the Fiscal Profile panel.

2

Input Micro-Spend: Enter a routine daily expense amount (like a coffee or subscription) in the Momentum panel.

3

Choose Momentum: Select either "- Impulse" to see what spending costs, or "+ Save" to see what investing yields.

4

Execute Scan: Click the central Probability Scan button to generate your personalized 10-year forensic projection.
