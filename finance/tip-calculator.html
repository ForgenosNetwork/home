2. The Math Behind Smart Rounding

One of the most requested features by finance professionals is "Smart Rounding". Have you ever looked at your bank statement and been annoyed by transactions like $43.87? Smart Rounding fixes this.

When you toggle "Round Total", the engine takes your subtotal and desired tip, calculates the raw total, and then forces that total to the nearest whole number (e.g., $44.00). It then reverse-engineers the math: New Tip = Rounded Total - Subtotal. This ensures the restaurant gets a fair tip, but your credit card statement remains impeccably clean. The same logic applies if you choose to round the tip directly, or round the per-person split to make requests easier.

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this tip calculator completely free?

Yes, this tool on Forgenos is 100% free. No signup, no subscription, no hidden fees.

How does the Smart Rounding feature work?

Smart Rounding allows you to force your total, tip, or per-person amount to a clean, whole dollar. The calculator automatically adjusts the tip percentage in the background to make the math perfect for a clean bank statement.

Is my financial data safe?

Absolutely. This tool runs 100% locally in your web browser. No bill amounts or totals are ever sent to a server or stored anywhere.

Can I calculate the tip before tax?

Yes. You can click "+ Add Tax Separately" to enter your tax amount, and use the Advanced Settings to choose whether the tip is calculated on the pre-tax subtotal or the post-tax total.

How many people can I split the bill with?

You can split the bill equally among up to 50 people using the interactive slider. The "Per Person" total will update instantly.
