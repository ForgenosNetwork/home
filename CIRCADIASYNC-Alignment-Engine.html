+0H OFFSET (ANCHOR)

--:--

Phase 2: Caffeine Start

+1.5H OFFSET (DELAY)

--:--

Allow adenosine to naturally clear before consuming caffeine. This strategic delay prevents the dreaded afternoon energy crash.

Phase 3: Dim Overhead Lights

+12H OFFSET (PREP)

--:--

Switch to low, warm-colored lighting (lamps). Avoid bright overhead lights to allow natural melatonin secretion to begin.

Phase 4: Fasting Close

+14H OFFSET (DIGEST)

--:--

Stop eating all calories. Digesting food too close to bedtime ruins deep sleep architecture and raises core body temperature.

+16H OFFSET (RECOVER)

--:--

Room should be cold and completely dark. Your body is now hormonally primed for restorative deep sleep and cellular repair.
